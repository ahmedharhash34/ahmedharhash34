# Ahmed Harhash - Cardiologist & AI Researcher

## About
Physician-Scientist at the University of Arizona Sarver Heart Center, specializing in the intersection of cardiovascular medicine and Artificial Intelligence. My research focuses on developing and validating deep learning models for high-stakes clinical applications, particularly in ECG interpretation and predictive analytics.

## Focus Areas
- **Deep Learning for ECG:** Developing CNN and Transformer-based models to detect Occlusion Myocardial Infarction (OMI) from standard 12-lead ECGs, outperforming traditional STEMI criteria.
- **Digital Echocardiography:** Applying machine learning to automate the acquisition and interpretation of ultrasound images for valve disease diagnosis.
- **Predictive Analytics in Resuscitation:** Utilizing ML models to predict neurological recovery and outcomes in survivors of out-of-hospital cardiac arrest.
- **AI Ethics & Validation:** Ensuring the robustness, interpretability, and safety of AI algorithms in clinical workflows.
